The annotations capture strong by the student’s teacher, using the features of quality, with a view to establishing the level of achievement the text reflects. The purpose of the annotations is to make the teacher's thinking visible. The annotations were confirmed by the Quality Assurance group, consisting of practicing English teachers and representatives of the Inspectorate, the SEC and JCT.
